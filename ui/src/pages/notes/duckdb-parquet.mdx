[some time now](/notes/clickhouse/). After a year or so of sticking to my ClickHouse based setup,
I'm back to it. This time the motivation is to eliminate the somewhat heavy DB from my homeserver.
Ideally I would have my own service store and query data without a DB server running. No queries, no
load.

fields are performant. My current solution heavily relies on the excellent Map type in Clickhouse.
